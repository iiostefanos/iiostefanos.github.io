How To Play

Objective:

'PLAY' button will start the game. First, the game will begin with a flash of 1 (one) of the 4 (four) colored sides from the board and will increase by 1 (one) on the number display with each pattern the player followed correctly. The goal of the Simon Game is to repeat the pattern that the computer has generated until the player will be able to reach the end of the game by scoring '20', the maximum, in order to be the WINNER of the Simon Game. So, if you think that you have a great skill and memory, play, stay focused and try not to make mistakes! Accept challenge ?!?

To Win:

  • Reaching to the score of '20' will make the player become the WINNER of the Simon Game.
  • However, if 'STRICT' mode is not activated and the player clicks to activate it, the number counter will start back at '0'.
  • The player can opt to switch off 'STRICT' mode at any time, not having to begin a new game.

Button:

PLAY - To start the Simon Game, just simply press 'PLAY'. If the player decides at some time to begin all over again will have to just click 'PLAY'.
